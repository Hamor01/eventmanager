MobiGirlz WSIS Award

#} {##} {#

MobiGirlz initiative made history as it was nominated for the prestigious WSIS Prize 2023, alongside 900 other worthy contenders. MobiGirlz emerged the winners in the Category, Enabling Environment during the Annual World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS).

#} {##} {#
Once in a Lifetime Achievement
#} {##} {#

The Summit was organized by International Telecommunications Union (ITU), United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) and United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD).

#} {##} {#
Champions Award at the UN Summit
#} {##} {#

eMobilis Co-Founder and Managing Director traveled to Geneva, Switzerland to receive the Champions Award at the UN Summit. This incredible achievement was a testament to the passion and hard work of the MobiGirlz team, whose mission is to create a more inclusive and diverse tech landscape.
